Chicken Piccata

What is Chicken Piccata?

Chicken Piccata is a lemony chicken dish that is saut√©ed with butter and chicken broth in a pan. It also usually contains capers and goes well with Caesar Salad or Garlic Bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 large skinless, goneless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 stick butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 3 medium lemons, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons drained capers
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
